What to do next

Key deadlines, what to gather, and where to go from here.

What to gather before you file

  • P60 — from your employer, shows gross pay and tax paid
  • P11D — if you have taxable benefits (company car, private medical etc.)
  • Bank interest statements — if above your Personal Savings Allowance
  • Rental income records — rent received and all allowable expenses
  • Dividend vouchers / broker statements — if you received dividends
  • Pension certificates — to claim higher rate relief on contributions
  • Gift Aid records — to claim higher rate relief on donations
  • UTR number — if registered, your 10-digit Unique Taxpayer Reference

Not registered yet?

If this is your first Self Assessment you need to register with HMRC. For the 2024/25 tax year the deadline to register is 5 October 2025. You can register at gov.uk/register-for-self-assessment. You’ll receive your UTR number within 10 working days.

Want help with your return? A tax adviser or accountant can file on your behalf and ensure you don’t pay more than you need to.
